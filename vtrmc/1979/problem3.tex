Let $A$ be an $n \times n$ nonsingular matrix with complex elements, and let
$\bar{A}$ be its complex conjugate. Let $B = A\bar{A} + I$, where $I$ is the $n
\times n$ identity matrix.

Prove or disprove:

\begin{enumerate}[(a)]
\item $A^{-1}BA = \bar{B}$
\item The determinant of $A \bar{A} + I$ is real.
\end{enumerate}
